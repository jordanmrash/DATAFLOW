    Template design




    8


    Suggestions for template design include:

  • Use of dynamic formulas where permissible

    • RefersToSheetName formula vs. hard coded name

    • Formulas to calculate row and column argument in hide formulas





  • Attach documents within request, as opposed to outside request




  • Linking functionality

    • Runtime formulas (DFGet)

    • Non-runtime formulas (ExtDFGet, ExtDFPut)



      Confidential and Proprietary


      Performance




      12


  • Minimize index/match, offset, arrays and other processor intensive formulas

  • Stack contact on sheets, as opposed to adding sheets due to performance issues with Excel 2013 and later versions

  • Row/column references should be to specific range, not entire row/column

  • HideRows and HideCols, in lieu of HideRow and HideCol

  • Utilize Last Cell Report
